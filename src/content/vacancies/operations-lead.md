We are seeking an experienced Operations Lead to support the Partners and HR/Operations Manager in the day-to-day running of the firm. This is a key role within our practice, providing essential operational support across a range of business functions.

## Key Responsibilities

- Providing operational support to the Partners and HR/Operations Manager in all aspects of firm management
- Supporting risk management processes, including maintaining the firm's risk register and ensuring appropriate controls are in place
- Assisting with regulatory compliance, including SRA requirements, anti-money laundering procedures, and data protection obligations
- Contributing to financial reporting, budgeting and management information
- Coordinating IT systems and liaising with external IT providers to ensure the firm's technology infrastructure meets business needs
- Supporting marketing activities, including website content management, social media presence and client communications
- Assisting with office management and facilities across both the Totnes and Kingsbridge offices
- Contributing to business development and practice improvement initiatives

## About You

You will be an organised, proactive and versatile professional with experience in an operational or management support role, ideally within a legal or professional services environment. You will be comfortable working across a range of functions and able to manage multiple priorities. Strong IT skills and excellent attention to detail are essential.

## Reporting

This role reports to the HR/Operations Manager.

## Benefits

- Competitive salary commensurate with experience
- Company pension scheme
- 24/7 Employee Assistance Programme
- Professional development and training support
- Generous holiday allowance
- Free or subsidised parking
