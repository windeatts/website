We are seeking an experienced Family Solicitor or Legal Executive to join our friendly, forward-thinking and client-focused firm. This is an excellent opportunity to manage your own caseload within a supportive and well-established practice.

## Key Responsibilities

- Managing an independent caseload of family law matters including divorce and separation, financial settlements, children matters, cohabitation disputes, and domestic abuse cases
- Providing clear, empathetic and practical legal advice to clients going through difficult personal circumstances
- Representing clients at court hearings and mediations
- Building and maintaining strong client relationships
- Contributing to the growth and reputation of the family law offering within the firm

## About You

You will have strong knowledge of family law and court procedures, with a proven track record of managing your own caseload. You will be a dedicated, compassionate and resilient professional who can provide excellent client care while managing the demands of a busy practice. Membership of Resolution would be advantageous.

## Working Arrangements

Hybrid working arrangements will be considered for the right candidate. We are committed to supporting a healthy work-life balance.

- Professional development and training support
- Generous holiday allowance
