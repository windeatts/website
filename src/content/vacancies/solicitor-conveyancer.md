We are seeking a qualified Solicitor or Licensed Conveyancer with a minimum of 3 years' post-qualification experience in residential and commercial property to join our busy and well-established Property department.

## Key Responsibilities

- Managing a varied caseload of residential and commercial property matters independently from instruction through to completion
- Providing timely, accurate and commercially aware advice to clients
- Handling billing and financial management of your case files
- Supporting and mentoring colleagues within the team
- Maintaining compliance with SRA regulations and internal quality standards
- Contributing to the development and growth of the department

## About You

You will be an experienced property lawyer who is confident managing your own caseload while working as part of a supportive team. You will have excellent client care skills and a strong attention to detail. Experience in both residential and commercial property work is preferred, though candidates with strong experience in one area and a willingness to develop the other will also be considered.

## Benefits

- Competitive salary commensurate with experience
- Company pension scheme
- 24/7 Employee Assistance Programme
- Professional development and training support
- Generous holiday allowance
- Free or subsidised parking
