Providing a calming influence when you need it.

Buying or selling a business can be stressful, particularly for first-time transactions. You'll encounter unfamiliar considerations such as business premises, staff matters, and technical terminology.

Our experienced lawyers will explain all concepts in straightforward language and guide you through all questions and issues throughout the transaction. We handle diverse commercial transactions, from shop lease takeovers to industrial unit purchases through to large multi-branch company acquisitions.

Where feasible, we work on fixed-fee arrangements, allowing you to budget legal costs as part of your overall expenses.

## Our Services

We provide a local, personal service with extensive commercial transaction experience. We handle:

- Drafting and reviewing all contracts and required documents
- Raising and answering business-related enquiries
- Liaison with your accountants to protect your interests

## Our Commitment

- Initial informal discussion available
- Fixed fee estimates where possible
- Local, qualified personal service
- Good channels of communication

## We Can Help With

- Sale or purchase of leases, or taking new leases
- Freehold shop and industrial unit transactions
- Restaurants, pubs, or clubs sales and purchases (including licensing assistance)
- Franchise arrangements
