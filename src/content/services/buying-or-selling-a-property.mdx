### Conveyancing you can trust

A first-rate, cost effective conveyancing service you can trust.

Buying or selling a property is often a time of great excitement, but there is usually a huge amount to think about, from arranging removals and insurance to remembering to take the guinea pig.

Whether you are buying or selling, we recognise the value of having a conveyancer you can trust to get on with all the legal formalities, leaving you free to deal with everything else. For generations we have provided an excellent personal service tailored to the needs of our clients. No two transactions are the same, and our fully qualified conveyancers will make sure that your best interests are protected every time.

We have a wide range of experience, and can help with selling and buying flats and houses, whether newly built or centuries old. We also regularly deal with right-to-buy and affordable housing as well as investment properties and buy-to-lets.

We will keep you informed of progress throughout, and will let you know as soon as any potential problems arise so there won't be any nasty last minute surprises. And we'll give you full information about the cost at the outset too.

Our aim is to build an understanding with you throughout your transaction so that at its conclusion, you feel that you have received a first-rate, cost effective service and that you would be happy to use our services again as well as recommend us to your friends, family and colleagues.

## A wide range of expertise

**Free initial discussion**
We are happy to talk to you either face to face or by telephone to sort out any initial queries you may have

**Clear charges**
We believe that we provide an excellent service for a competitive fee and do not charge for hidden extras. We make it clear at the start of your transaction what these will be and, should any unexpected additional work be required we shall let you know immediately

**Expertise**
All of our conveyancers are highly specialised in property matters and we have a dedicated team of support staff. Together we aim to make your move as swift and easy as we can

**Clear communication**
It is essential that you are kept in the loop throughout your transaction. We ensure that the same person will handle it from start to finish and that you will be regularly updated by telephone, e-mail and letter or one-to one meeting

**Latest technology**
We make full use of the online service provided by Local Authorities, Water and Environmental Search providers and the Land Registry wherever possible to speed up your transaction.
