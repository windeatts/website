Julie was born in Torquay and began her career as an office junior in a local firm of solicitors. In 1998, Julie became a secretary in the conveyancing department at Bynes Solicitors and continued to progress, qualifying as a Licensed Conveyancer in 2008.

In her spare time Julie enjoys swimming, golf and taking walks with her dog around the local area. Julie became a partner in 2020.
