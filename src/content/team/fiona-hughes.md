Fiona joined Windeatts in 1993 and was admitted as a Fellow of the Institute of Legal Executives in 2001. She became a solicitor in 2002, a Partner in 2005 and now co-manages both offices.

Fiona has extensive experience in Personal Injury and general Civil Litigation, including Landlord and Tenant, property disputes, trusts disputes for cohabitees and general contract matters. Fiona also specialises in probate disputes and is a member of the Association of Contentious Trust and Probate Specialists.
