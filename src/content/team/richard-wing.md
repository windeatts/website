Richard is a Partner and Solicitor who specialises in commercial and residential conveyancing. He has in-depth experience of residential transactions and has acted for a wide variety of high net worth individuals in the sale and purchase of both freehold and leasehold properties.

He also deals with re-mortgages, shared ownership, help to buy, new builds, deeds of variation and deeds of surrender and re-grant of leases, plot sales on small and larger developments, high value property purchases and complicated title purchase.

He has dealt with business sales and purchases covering a wide range of sectors including retail and marine and is happy to discuss your matter on an informal basis prior to instruction.

In Richard's spare time he enjoys keeping fit by cycling and running around the beautiful countryside in the South Hams.
